Take your pick.  Films releasing this weekend: November 28, 2014 /films-releasing-this-weekend-november-28-2014/eventshow/45295022.cms 02 Ungli: Ungli is a story of a five friends who turn rebels after being fed up from the corrupt system. They decide to take the law in their own hands and fix the system in their own style. They form an organized Ungli gang which kidnaps officers and punishes them to teach them a lesson. As big names like Kangna Ranaut, Emraan Hashmi, Randeep Hooda along with Arunoday Singh and Neil Bhoopalam come together to change the system, they have to face Sanjay Dutt, the police commissioner. The film is a masala entertainer and promises a lot. And then you also have 'Dance Basanti' Shraddha Kapoor to cool your nerves.  Films releasing this weekend: November 28, 2014 /films-releasing-this-weekend-november-28-2014/eventshow/45295014.cms 03 The Hunger Games: Mockingjay - Name the Hunger Games series and the adventure junkies get excited. The Hunger Games: Mockingjay is the first of the two-part final series of The Hunger Games trilogy. The film picks up with Katniss Everdeen (Jennifer Lawrence) who turns a reluctant hero but a sign of hope for Panem after escaping the Third Quarter Quell. She finds herself in District 13 after finally putting an end to the Games. Under the guidance of President Coin and the advice of her trusted friends, Katniss spreads her wings to save Peeta and fulfill the hope of her country Panem.  Films releasing this weekend: November 28, 2014 /films-releasing-this-weekend-november-28-2014/eventshow/45294982.cms 04 Penguins of Madagascar: Penguins of Madagascar is a 3D computer-animated action comedy film revolving around penguins and their journey from an iceberg to a circus while they are kidnapped, rescued, undergoing experiences they never dream of. It revolves around three penguins who save an egg from hungry seals and together they become a team. Loaded with several twists and turns including their kidnapping by an octopus, their rescue by a wolf named Classified (Benedict Cumberbatch) and his team. Penguins of Madagascar is expected to be pure fun with not even a single wasted moment.  Films releasing this weekend: November 28, 2014 /films-releasing-this-weekend-november-28-2014/eventshow/45294967.cms 05 Horrible Bosses 2: Horrible Bosses 2 is a comedy film starring the best combo of comic experts like Jason Bateman, Christoph Waltz, Jason Sudeikis and Jennifer Aniston. Horrible Bosses 2 is the story of three friends who turn their own bosses by cracking a business idea. But they are challenged by a savvy investor and therefore decide to kidnap his son for ransom. How they attempt the kidnapping and do they fall into their own trap can be worth a watch. Comedy always has many takes when it comes to one like Horrible Bosses 2.  Films releasing this weekend: November 28, 2014 /films-releasing-this-weekend-november-28-2014/eventshow/45294906.cms 06 Zed Plus: Zed Plus is a satire drama set in a sleepy town in Rajasthan. The unique story revolves around a dargah’s caretaker who is provided the most coveted Z-plus security by the prime minister who visits the dargah. How his life changes from a common man, particularly a 'puncture-wala,' to a celebrity who is followed by several guards even while going to relieve himself, is an out of the box story. Starring Mona Singh as the leading lady, this flick can be a different one with a punch of comedy.  Films releasing this weekend: November 28, 2014 /films-releasing-this-weekend-november-28-2014/eventshow/45294899.cms 07 Zid: Priyanka Chopra’s much talked about cousin Barbie Handa now called Mannara, is making her Bollywood debut with erotic thriller Zid. Turning an obsessive lover in the film, Mannara will be seen scorching the silver screen with her sizzling appearance and interesting role. The actress may keep the temperatures high in the movie theatres as the film is loaded with intimate songs, suspense and romance. This one can be a good pick for those looking for a not-so-perfect love story.  Films releasing this weekend: November 28, 2014 /films-releasing-this-weekend-november-28-2014/eventshow/45296574.cms 08 Hum Hai Teen Khurafaati: Waiting since long for a spicy adult comedy like Grand Masti? Flock to watch Hum Hai Teen Khurafaati which is releasing this weekend. It is the story of three friends looking out for some fun who will go to any extent in search of it.
