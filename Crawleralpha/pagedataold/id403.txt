Box Office Specials Celeb Blogs You are here: Home » Entertainment » Hindi » Bollywood Breaking News: Celebrities at Arpita Khan and Aayush Sharma’s reception 1/14 01 That Shah Rukh shook a leg with Salman Khan at the latter's sister's wedding reception in Mumbai, is by now a well-known fact. However, SRK's energy on the dance floor is definitely worth a mention. According to a report in Mumbai Mirror, the actor danced non-stop for over an hour stopping only for a sip of water before being dragged back on the dance floor by Huma Qureshi and brother Saquib Salim for some more naach-gaana. He began by dragging the recently-wed couple on the dance floor and continued to dance much after they had excused themselves. The actor danced to songs from his film, being belted out by an excited DJ. Songs like Baazigar's 'Kali kali aankhen,' Chennai Express's 'Lungi Dance' and Happy New Year's 'Indiawale' kept the superstar grooving. However, it was when Kuch Kuch Hota Hai number 'Mujhko kya hua...' was played that the crowd went beserk at SRK's moves. Many oldies also joined in on the dance floor to shake a leg with the Baadshah Khan. Seeing Shah Rukh's rather enthusiastic dance, Arbaaz and Sohail joined him, followed by Pulkit Samrat with wife and Salman's rakhi sister Shweta and Dolly Ki Doli director Abhishek Dogra. Rajkummar Rao, his girlfriend Patralekha, Dia Mirza and hubby Sahil Sangha also made an appearance. Surprisingly, Hrithik showed off his dance moves only for a while and Ajay Devgn chose not to at all. However, Sonakshi let her hair down and partied with the rest.
