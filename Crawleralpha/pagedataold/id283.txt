According to a report in Hindustan Times, the actor is taking a paternity leave. In the report, a source has been quoted as saying that Riteish has been by his wife's side ever since she was admitted to the hospital and was planning to resume shooting only after two weeks. The report also states that Genelia will be discharged from the hospital in a week's time. Meanwhile, the doting new father is getting impatient for his wife and son's homecoming and apparently so is their dog. The actor shared an image of their dog, Flash, waiting eagerly by the elevator and wrote, "FLASH is waiting for mommy @geneliad to come back home."
