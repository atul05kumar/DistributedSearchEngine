And in this season, the end of first week was very eventful. Everyone in the house was given the first team task where one team would try to capture seats from the other team. The sportive spirit of the game took a rather unexpected turn when Deepshikha's team began chafing chili powder and paste on the faces and bodies of the members of the other team. Gautam Gulati couldn't take it anymore and his rage of outburst showed up when he abused Karishma. However Gautam later pleaded for forgiveness, but for now the two are not great friends.  Bigg Boss 8: Highlights of this season /bigg-boss-8-highlights-of-this-season/eventshow/45284782.cms 03 Aarya Babbar became one of the highlights of the show. The actor revealed about his alleged affair with Minissha Lamba on the show. Both were contestants on Bigg Boss 8 and Aarya spoke openly about his personal life on national television. Apparently, Aarya had asked Minissha to fake romance on the show but she had turned it down and discussed it with the inmates. And when host Salman Khan questioned the two about the same, Aarya revealed that they had been in a relationship earlier, however he later apologized to Minissha for speaking about their past.  Bigg Boss 8: Highlights of this season /bigg-boss-8-highlights-of-this-season/eventshow/45284778.cms 04 Several celebrities visit the sets of this popular show to promote their film. However Hrithik Roshan was the one who entertained a lot with his quick presence on the show. While initially Hrithik was not going to make any appearances on television shows for promotions, he decided otherwise for Bigg Boss. As a part of promotions for Bang Bang, he gave the contestants a challenge to walk over a pile of broken glass. Hrithik Roshan also treated all the contestants with some amazing cupcakes and a royal dinner. He spent the afternoon enjoying with the contestants of the house.  Bigg Boss 8: Highlights of this season /bigg-boss-8-highlights-of-this-season/eventshow/45284781.cms 05 Bigg Boss house always celebrates festivals with great enthusiasm. And this year too, the festival of Diwali was one of the biggest highlights of the season. Salman Khan made a rather dramatic entry in the house. Also present on the occasion was renowned magician Hussain Rizvi. The contestants seemed to have a great time with Salman Khan who played truth and dare with them. What followed were special gifts for everyone from their families. However the gifts didn't end there as the generous host had brought personalized 'Being Human' goodie bags too for all the contestants. This was followed by some great dance as Salman Khan enthusiastically shook a leg with everyone.  Bigg Boss 8: Highlights of this season /bigg-boss-8-highlights-of-this-season/eventshow/45284773.cms 06 Dimpy Mahajan had tied the knot with Rahul Mahajan who is an ex-contestant from the reality show. However after four years of togetherness, the duo filed for divorce and Dimpy entered the Bigg Boss house as a wild-card entrant. According to a report on dna.com, Dimpy had been discussing her marriage and impending divorce. The report further stated that Dimpy wanted to move out of the emotional turmoil that she was facing and hence the reality show.  Bigg Boss 8: Highlights of this season /bigg-boss-8-highlights-of-this-season/eventshow/45284771.cms 07 Renee is a popular contestant from the reality show Roadies, she had come across as a strong contender there too. And in the Bigg Boss house, Renee comes across as a bit aggressive contestant and has formed good friendship with Upen Patel in the house. However, recently she surprised everyone with a rather bold statement on national television. Salman Khan who comes on the show during weekends was seen mocking at her, and Renee said, "I want to kill you Salman for this."
