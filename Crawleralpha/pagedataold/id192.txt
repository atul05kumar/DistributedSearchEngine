Computer Science > Computer Science and Game Theory Title: Phutball is PSPACE-hard Authors: Dariusz Dereniowski (Submitted on 10 Apr 2008 (v1), last revised 20 Mar 2009 (this version, v2)) Abstract: We consider the $n\times n$ game of Phutball. It is shown that, given an arbitrary position of stones on the board, it is a PSPACE-hard problem to determine whether the specified player can win the game, regardless of the opponent's choices made during the game.
