Get Access Abstract We consider planar circuits, formulas and multilective planar circuits. It is shown that planar circuits and formulas are incomparable. An Ω(n logn) lower bound is given for the multilective planar circuit complexity of a decision problem and an Ω(n 3/2) lower bound is given for the multilective planar circuit complexity of a multiple output function.
