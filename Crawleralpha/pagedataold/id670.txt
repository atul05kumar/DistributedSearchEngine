Meanwhile, a number of problem are posed for further research. LIU Yanpei is a Professor of Northern Jiaotong University. His research work are mainly on the theory of enumerating maps on surfaces and combinatorial optimization related to graphs, networks and Boolean functions especially appearing in VLSI circuit designs and CAD.
