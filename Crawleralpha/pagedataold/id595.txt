Sinceω<2.38, this algorithm breaks through theO(n 1.5) barrier for the matching problem. This is the first result of this kind for general planar graphs. We also present an algorithm for generating perfect matchings in planar graphs uniformly at random usingO(n ω/2) arithmetic operations. Our algorithms are based on the Gaussian elimination approach to maximum matchings introduced in [16]. This research was supported by KBN Grant 4T11C04425.
