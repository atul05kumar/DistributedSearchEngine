Things took an ugly turn as Gautam had an outburst and vented against Karishma when he could not bear the chili anymore and hurled abuses towards her. The fight turned so ugly that the all the other housemates turned against Gautam for abusing Karishma. These big fights not only help the show to boost its numbers but, at times, also becomes a topic of discussion everywhere. Let’s take a look at some of the biggest fights inside the Bigg Boss house across all seasons. Kushal-Andy: During the 'ignore the obvious' task, Kushal got into a violent fight with Andy, when Andy passed some unpleasant comment about Gauahar. Andy was asked to irritate and disturb the housemates during the task with his antics and in return, the house mates were asked to ignore him. However, the task got ugly when Andy passed some derogatory comments on Gauahar's lingerie. Andy's comments irked Gauahar and Kushal. He immediately rushed to Andy and tried to hit him but other housemates including Gauahar stopped him. However, Kushal didn't stop and kept abusing Andy. Soon Bigg Boss made an announcement and asked Kushal to pack his bags and leave the house owing to his violent behavior.
